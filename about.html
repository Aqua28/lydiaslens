Behind the Lens

Hi! Welcome to lydiaslens! This is Lydia Liu, part-time high school student, part-time nature photographer and creator of this website. All the the photos on this website are mine. I love being in nature and hope you will join me in preserving these natural places.

My images have been featured by Yosemite National Park and various photography organizations' socia media platforms. When I'm not out in the mountains, you will find me exploring the beautiful city of San Francisco!



Here you will find photos from my latest adventures. My work focuses primarily on nature and landscape photography. Check out my Instagram profile for more.This website is still a work in progress. Email me if you've got feedback. I'd love to hear from you!
